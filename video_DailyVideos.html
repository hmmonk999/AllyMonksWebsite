Camp Ozark: Daily Videos


Everyday at camp, the video team creates 2 minute long video that showcases 3-5 seconds clips of that day's events. The video team works together to film clips of campers doing various activies thoughout the day. As a video editor for the daily videos, I had half an hour to watch all the clips and piece together an entertaining video to show campers after dinner.

My Team

  • Me and 3 Other Videographers
  • 1 Video Editors
  • 1.5 hours editing

Time Taken

  • 6-7 hours filming
  • 1.5 hours editing

The Technology

  • Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Ronin S, Ronin M
  • Canon 80D
  • Drone
  • Wide Angle, Super Telephoto, and Super Telephoto Lenses

The Process

goal: 2 minute video show the highlights of each days

audience: worship cut, then release to parents

requiments: team comps (sports), morning activies (structures, sign up for classes indoor or outdoors), mishmash (unstructed), special event (theme night shit)

filming: shoot manually, have adjust settings based on lighting,

editing: make each section 30s long ()

other: mish-mash - unstructured free time (assigned to areas, ropes water,

filming: film at the most populated areas, film the most excited camper, first time camper, kids who look happy,

staged filming: reaction shots of a group of kids, analyze what the kids what to do to film stuff, film as much as possible (deleted 3000 videos during 1 week)
