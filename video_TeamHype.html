Camp Ozark: Team Hype Video


Team hype videos highlight the differnt athletic events, like relay races, handball, or waterpolo, from team competitions in the morning. I used a Ronin S and Canon 80D to film action shots and in-the-moment recactions of the campers playing and winning games.

My Team

  • Me and 3 Other Videographers
  • 1 Video Editors
  • 1.5 hours editing

Time Taken

  • 50 minutes for filming
  • 15-20 minutes fot editing

The Technology

  • Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Ronin S
  • Canon 80D
  • DJI Osmo Action

The Process

To get these videos finished, we had 50 mintues to film team competitions and about 20 minutes to edit the video. We would show the video to campers the morning after. Our goal was to inspire campers with team bonding, cheering, and the hype vibe so they would play hard for that day's team competitions. This video had to have an even ratio of boys to girls and age ranges.

When deciding where to film for team competitions, I made sure to analyze which campers were playing what sport and where I thought the most action would happen. I wanted to make sure that I could capture the most interesting parts of whatever game was being played that day. As videographers we were running around with cameras in the middle of games, but we also had to make sure that we didn't interfere with any of the campers playing. Being aware of where all the players are at any time while trying to find the best video angle was an interesting and fun challege to tackle.
