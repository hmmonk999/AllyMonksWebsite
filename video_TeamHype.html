Camp Ozark: Team Hype Video


Team hype videos highlight the differnt athletic events, like relay races, handball, or waterpolo, from team competitions in the morning. I used a Ronin S and Canon 80D to film action shots and in-the-moment recactions of the campers playing and winning games.

My Team

  • Me and 3 Other Videographers
  • 1 Video Editors
  • 1.5 hours editing

Time Taken

  • 50 minutes for filming
  • 15-20 minutes fot editing

The Technology

  • Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Ronin S
  • Canon 80D
  • DJI Osmo Action

The Process

team: me and 3 other videographers filmed, each member filmed a differnt sport

goal: get as many shots of people scoring as possible and team bonding, cheering, and the hype vibe

video need to include: even ratio of boys/girls and age groups (5-9, 10-13, 14-17)

deliverable: 50 second video

editing: add transitions (pre-made) based on sounds, music is the same every video and timestamps where premade transions occur

audience: just shown to the campers in a morning meeting the following day

team leaders are filmed at the beginning of the video

why did i pick where to shoot: I chose angles on where to shoot that would best depict the best athletes and team spirits on each team and analyzed where in the game people scored from so I could get the best angle. You have to run around a ton, get in between the action while avoiding interfering
