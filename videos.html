.01

Camp Ozark: One Week Session Video

At Camp Ozark, campers attend sessions for one week. As a videographer, my biggest challenge was to capture all events of the week for all ages of campers, 5-17, and tell a cohesive story full of fun and memories for campers and their parents to share after the camp ended.



.02

Camp Ozark: Daily Videos

Everyday at camp, the video team creates 2 minute long video that showcases 3-5 seconds clips of that day's events. The video team works together to film clips of campers doing various activies thoughout the day. As a video editor for the daily videos, I had half an hour to watch all the clips and piece together an entertaining video to show campers after dinner.



.03

Camp Ozark: Team Hype Video

Team hype videos highlight the differnt athletic events, like relay races, handball, or waterpolo, from team competitions in the morning. I used a Ronin S and Canon 80D to film action shots and in-the-moment recactions of the campers playing and winning games.
